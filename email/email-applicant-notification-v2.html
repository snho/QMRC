Course Information

To allow time for registration, paperwork and fee payment, please arrive 30-60 minutes before the course start time!

Aside from your course fees, you will also need to bring suitable photo identification - this includes a valid Australian drivers license / 18+ Card, or a valid passport.

Course materials will be provided, as well as Tea & Coffee and light refreshments (snacks). The course carries over lunch, so it is recommended to bring something for lunch, or alternatively the QRA Club nearby, offers great meals at a reasonable cost.

You will also be partaking in an practical shooting test, conducted outdoors on the range. We recommend bringing a hat, and sunscreen. Practical, closed footwear is required for safety reasons. It is recommended some sort of eye protection be worn - this can be glasses, sunglasses, or safety glasses.

For hearing protection, the QMRC can provide disposable earplugs, or you can bring noise reducing earmuffs if you find them more comfortable.
